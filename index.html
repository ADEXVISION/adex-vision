Enhance Your Multiplayer Experience

X-Coin Points

X-Coin Points are the official in-game currency of ADEX VISION, designed to enhance your overall multiplayer experience and unlock premium content across the platform.

With X-Coin Points, players can access exclusive cosmetics, seasonal items, special packs, Battle Pass upgrades, and future in-game features. All content is purely cosmetic or progression-based and never provides competitive advantages.

Earn X-Coin Points through gameplay, challenges and seasonal rewards, or purchase them directly to instantly unlock premium content. Battle Pass owners can also use X-Coins to unlock tiers faster and gain immediate access to epic seasonal rewards.

X-Coin Points are designed to support fair play while giving players the freedom to customize their experience and support the ongoing development of ADEX VISION.
