FAQ & Help

How do I purchase packs?

You can purchase packs directly in the Store section using supported payment methods.

I didn’t receive my purchase. What should I do?

Please contact our support via email or Discord with your transaction details.

How do I change my language?

You can change your language at the bottom of the Store or in the Support section.

Where can I report bugs?

You can report bugs via email or directly in our Discord support channels.
